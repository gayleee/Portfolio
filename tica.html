About TICA

TICA: A Technological Innovation for Communication in Apraxia - A Mobile Application Utilizing AI-Driven Speech Therapy for Children with Apraxia was a significant project for my colleagues and me, as it provided the opportunity for direct, face-to-face communication with our client, DreamLab Therapy Center in San Pablo City, Laguna, to discuss user requirements. As both UI/UX designers and front-end developers, we dedicated considerable time to this project, learning new mobile design principles and a new framework (Kivy and KivyMD) along the way. Through iterative design based on client feedback, we successfully completed the project.

Building TICA: The Why Behind the What

Our design choices were driven by our client's core needs. These details our exploration of font, color, and mascot selections. We began by merging our client's vision and a deep understanding of our target users. Through iterative design and extensive research, we achieved a balance between vibrancy and accessibility. The creation of our mascots involved extensive sketching and conceptualization, ensuring the character effectively conveyed a relatable and engaging vibe for the user. Every decision was made with the dual goal of satisfying our client's needs and creating a intuitive user experience, resulting in a functional and engaging design system.

Behind the Interface: TICA's System Logic

As mentioned earlier, my colleague and I were not only the UI/UX designers for this project, but also the front-end developers. The following pages showcase key areas of the app, where we invested significant time in design and development iterations. We carefully considered the capabilities and limitations of Kivy and KivyMD, the Python GUI framework we used, ensuring that our design goals were achievable within these constraints.

Let's delve into how the chapter and quiz unlock system functions. Each chapter corresponds to a stage in the articulation hierarchy, starting with Chapter 1, the Discrimination level, where users learn to differentiate sounds. The chapters progress sequentially, culminating in the Words level. Quizzes are unlocked upon completion of their corresponding chapters (Chapter 1 unlocks Quiz 1). This chapter-quiz pairing is the core logic of the learning system.
