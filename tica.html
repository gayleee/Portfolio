TICA KEY PAGES

TICA was a significant project for my colleagues and me, as it provided the opportunity for direct, face-to-face communication with our client, DreamLab Therapy Center in San Pablo City, Laguna, to discuss user requirements. As both UI/UX designers and front-end developers, we dedicated considerable time to this project, learning new mobile design principles and a new framework along the way. Through iterative design based on client feedback, we successfully completed the project.
