About PAL

Positive Aid Link (PAL), a mental health scheduling and service platform developed in collaboration with HealSpace Psychological Clinic in Lipa, Batangas, marked my first experience designing UI/UX for a real-world application. As both UI/UX designers and front-end developers, my colleague and I collaborated and divided design responsibilities. He focused on the user-side (the clinic's clients), while I managed the administrative and consultant interfaces. We worked closely with our back-end developer and documentation team to ensure system functionality and resolve any issues.

This was also my first time designing for an established entity, requiring careful consideration of and alignment with their existing brand identity. I gained significant experience in communicating user requirements and planning page connectivity.

Building PAL: The Why Behind the What

Our design choices were guided by the goal of meeting the needs of both the clinic staff and their clients. We explored various font and color options, carefully considering the clinic's established identity to achieve a balance between brand alignment and user accessibility.
