About SHIFT

Due to time constraints, this project was reduced from a full-stack website to a front-end landing page only. However, I gained valuable experience in high-fidelity wireframing. My process includes problem analysis, information definition, low and high-fidelity wireframing, prototyping, and iterative refinement based on testing.

Building SHIFT: The Why Behind the What

This solo project allowed me full creative control. To align with the Olympic brand identity requirement, I chose a sporty aesthetic. The primary colors were selected for contrast and to differentiate from the Olympic palette, suggesting a brand collaboration rather than a direct imitation.

Behind the Interface: SHIFT's System Logic

To optimize loading times, I intended to reduce product categories and limit development scope. Additionally, the conceptual system design included enforcing user registration prior to cart access, aligning with standard e-commerce platforms. However, due to time constraints, this feature remained conceptual.
